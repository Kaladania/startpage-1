Keyboard Commands

Feeds Shift

  • left arrow toggle feed view.
  • right arrow toggle feed view.
  • Shift + 1 my Instapaper bookmarks.
  • Shift + 2 News feed (aggregated news).
  • Shift + 3 New York Times news.
  • Shift + 4 Reddit news.
  • Shift + 5 Podcasts
  • Shift + 6 Music
  • Shift + 7 YouTube latest videos.
  • Shift + 8 my Path of Exile characters.
  • Shift + 9 Web3 news.
  • Shift + 0 Developer news.
  • Shift + - Lexichronic news.
  • Shift + = NFT collection.

Media Controls Shift

  • Shift + Up increase audio speed.
  • Shift + Down decrease audio speed.
  • Shift + Right fast forward 15 seconds.
  • Shift + Left rewind 5 seconds.
  • Shift + space play/pause.
  • Shift + m mute audio.
  • Shift + v audio volume.
  • Shift + f12 play one random song.
  • Shift + f11 play shuffled playlist.
  • Shift + f10 play limited shuffled playlist.
  • Shift + f9 play 5 hours of ambience.
  • Shift + s skip song.
  • Shift + f fullscreen video.
  • Shift + t change music player.
  • esc exit fullscreen video.

Search Toggles Option

  • Option + 1 PoE Wiki
  • Option + 2 YouTube
  • Option + 3 DuckDuckGo
  • Option + 4 Apple Music
  • Option + 5 LastFM
  • Option + 6 Twitter
  • Option + 7 Google News
  • Option + 8 Github
  • Option + 9 Google
  • Option + 0 MidJourney

Settings Shift resets on refresh

  • Shift + z Main Menu toggles.
  • Shift + x fetch LastFM data.
  • Shift + c change background resolution.
  • Shift + b blur background.
  • Shift + n Wallet stats logged.
  • Shift + , view new background.
  • Shift + . toggle resized feed images.
  • Shift + / toggle resized container.
  • Shift + h help shortcuts menu.
  • Shift + Backspace toggle custom cursor.
